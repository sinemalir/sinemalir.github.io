Description

This analysis visualises parent- and student-submitted Google Form responses to uncover strategic insights on preferred lesson times, topic gaps, expectations and suggestions.

What does it deliver?

  • Clearly highlights topics students struggle with the most.
  • Identifies time slots with the highest demand for lessons.
  • Groups expectations and requests so curricula can be personalised.
  • Turns open-ended suggestions into actionable insights.
  • Builds trust by creating a data-driven feedback loop between educator, parent and student.

Outcome: An interactive roadmap for learner-centred, impact-driven education services.

Recommendations

  • Schedule live classes in the most requested time slots.
  • Tackle high-gap topics first (e.g., fractions, problem solving).
  • Diversify materials (summaries, practice sheets, video lessons) based on stated expectations.
  • Send weekly progress reports to meet parents‚Äô tracking needs.
  • Prototype gamification ideas (badges, point systems) voiced by students.
  • Re-run the survey regularly to track changes over time.
