Project 4 ‚Äì GA4 User Journey Analysis (Tableau + BigQuery)

Overview (Value-Driven, Client-Friendly)

This analysis tracks website or app traffic with Google Analytics 4. By mapping every step from the first interaction (e.g. landing page) to the final conversion (e.g. purchase), it boosts conversion rates and refines user experience.

What do you gain?

  • Answers where visitors come from, which pages they engage with and where they drop off.
  • Reveals the most common paths users take, clarifying real-world behaviour.
  • Identifies traffic sources that deliver the highest conversions.
  • Visualises the top-grossing devices, locations and channels at a glance.
  • Retention analysis delivers crystal-clear insights into customer loyalty.

Bottom line: A powerful tool for brands seeking to optimise marketing spend, raise conversion rates and turn visitors into loyal customers.

Data-Driven Recommendations

  • Allocate more budget to high-converting traffic sources.
  • Fix the most-abandoned pages (page speed, content, CTAs).
  • Serve localised content & campaigns in regions with strong loyalty.
  • Optimise mobile performance if metrics lag behind desktop.
  • Run retargeting for users who exit before key pages.
  • Improve onboarding flows for segments with low retention.
