Project 3 – Game Insights Dashboard (Tableau)

🔍 TECHNICAL ANALYSIS

1. Usage by Game Activity Type

  • Battle Pass Challenge in Game 3 dominates with nearly 100M seconds
  • Followed by “Main Campaign” and “Clan Multiplayer”
  • “Mini-games” are played far less

Players spend more time in large-scale competitive activities

2. Age Distribution vs Playtime

  • Young users (especially 15–25) play much more
  • After age 30, playtime drops significantly
  • The older the player, the shorter and less frequent the sessions

Young segment is clearly the most engaged group

3. Activity Over Time

  • Gameplay increases from March to December
  • August–October peak → likely due to summer break + new content
  • “Battle Pass” is consistently the top-played mode

Seasonal events are the main retention drivers

4. Device Age and Engagement

  • New device users are far more active
  • Old device users are significantly less active

Possibly due to performance issues or lack of compatibility

🎯 Recommendations

  • Optimize for old devices or guide users with outdated models
  • Redesign mini-games to improve interest
  • Target 15–25 age group in campaigns and game updates
  • Include device model in gameplay analytics and A/B testing

🎮 Summary (Non-technical)

🧠 What are players doing most?

  • Most time spent on competitive and seasonal events
  • Mini-games receive far less attention

Players prefer battles, teams, and big challenges

👦 Which age group is most active?

  • 15–25 age group spends the most time in-game
  • Older players spend less and less time

Younger players have a stronger bond with the game

📅 How does playtime change over time?

  • Playtime peaks during summer months
  • Holidays increase in-game engagement

Releasing new content around holidays can boost usage

📱 Device matters?

  • Newer phones/tablets → more active play
  • Older models → less activity

Could be due to lag, performance, or incompatibility
