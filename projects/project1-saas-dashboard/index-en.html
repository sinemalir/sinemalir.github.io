Project 1 ‚Äì SaaS Revenue Analysis (Tableau)

Description

This analysis is designed to help visualize revenue sources in a subscription-based (SaaS) business model more clearly, support strategic decisions with data, and make growth sustainable.

What does this analysis offer?

  • It clearly identifies which products generate the most revenue. This allows you to shape product strategy based on solid data and invest more in high-performing products.
  • It visualizes which geographies generate revenue, helping focus marketing and sales efforts in the right regions.
  • It shows changes in revenue over time, helping you catch seasonal trends and take timely actions.
  • With metrics like ARPPU (Average Revenue Per Paying User), it enables reevaluation of pricing strategies or the creation of new offers for different segments.
  • Analyzing revenue by user segments provides a foundation for loyalty programs, cross-sell campaigns, or premium conversion strategies.

Summary: This analysis is a powerful guide not only to understand today‚Äôs revenue but to plan and drive future growth with data.

Recommendations

  • Top-performing products should be promoted further rather than discounted. Success patterns can be applied to other products.
  • Local campaigns, payment options, and support should be implemented in regions with weak revenue.
  • Loyalty programs should be introduced for users with high ARPPU; these users can be offered exclusive content and early access.
  • Time-limited trials and personalized email campaigns are recommended to convert freemium users to premium.
  • Sales teams should be reinforced during seasonal peaks (e.g., year-end, early summer).
  • Premium users' behaviors should be analyzed for segment-based marketing strategies.

Technical Analysis

  • Tool Used: Tableau Public
  • Data Structure: User, revenue, and product data from a SaaS company

Dashboard Components:

  • Time-Based Revenue Chart (Line Chart)
    • Created using SUM(revenue_amount) over payment_date
    • Seasonal effects and campaign periods were detected
  • Product-Based Revenue (Bar Chart)
    • Bar chart sorted by SUM(revenue_amount) per product_name
    • Top revenue-generating products clearly highlighted
  • Country-Based Map Visualization (Map Chart)
    • SUM(revenue_amount) by country
    • Geo-comparative display of income
  • ARPPU KPI Card
    • Formula: SUM(revenue_amount) / COUNTD(paying_user_id)
  • Segment-Based User Distribution (Treemap / Donut)
    • SUM(revenue_amount) by user_segment
    • Freemium vs. premium user contributions analyzed separately

Interactive Features: All charts include dynamic filters and tool-tips for deep user insights.
